Future Cities Design Summit

The "Future Cities Design Summit" is a dynamic and visionary gathering that explores the limitless potential of urban design and sustainable city planning. In a rapidly changing world, where cities are at the forefront of transformation, this summit serves as a beacon for architects, urban planners, designers, and innovators, as well as all those passionate about the future of our urban environments.

This event is scheduled for November 10-12, 2023 at Bilkent University, Odeon. The event will begin from 9:00 am and end at 5:00pm. Seating is limited so be sure to secure your spot early!

GUEST SPEAKERS

Bjarke Ingels

Bjarke Ingels Group
UPC

Bjarke Ingels is celebrated for his innovative and sustainable designs, including the "8 House" and "VIA 57 West." He blends creativity with sustainability, reshaping modern architecture. His work redefines urban spaces, making them both visually stunning and environmentally responsible.

MORE INFO

Elizabeth Diller

Diller Scofidio+Renfro
The Cooper Union

Elizabeth Diller is the co-founder of Diller Scofidio + Renfro, renowned for her transformative architectural creations, including New York City's High Line and The Broad Museum in Los Angeles. Her innovative designs harmonize art and functionality in urban landscapes.

MORE INFO

Thom Mayne

Morphosis Architects
Harvard University

Thom Mayne is a distinguished architect known for his contributions to modern architecture. As the founder of Morphosis, his designs, such as the Cooper Union Building and the Perot Museum of Nature and Science, exemplify a fusion of sustainability, functionality, and aesthetic brilliance.

MORE INFO

Jeanne Gang

Studio Gang
The Illinois School of Architecture

Jeanne Gang is a celebrated architect and co-founder of Studio Gang, recognized for her visionary designs such as the Aqua Tower and the United States Embassy. Her work combines bold and sustainable architecture, demonstrating a unique approach to shaping the built environment.
